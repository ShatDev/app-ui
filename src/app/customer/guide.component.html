How to stake on TosDis?

  • 1: Install MetaMask Wallet in your Browser
  • 2: Connect MetaMask with TosDis Platform.
  • 3: Click "Stake" Button in pool
  • 4: Type in Token Amount
  • 5: Confirm transaction in MetaMask
  • 6: You can see your reward yields
  • 7: Click on Harvest to collect your rewards.
  • 8: Click on "Unstake" to withdraw Tokens

Pool Creation Guide

1. Open the "Create Pool" tab on Tosdis App, Select your preferred network, and connect your wallet.

2. Fill in all the parameters carefully as explained below, and click on "Approve DIS"

  • Staking Token: Address of token that users will stake in your pool.
  • Pool Token: Address of token that users will get as reward from your pool.
  • Start/Finish Timestamps: Go to https://www.unixtimestamp.com/ to calculate your pool start and end times.
  • Pool token supply: Enter the number of tokens that you want to distribute.

3. "Confirm" the transaction on your wallet and wait for it to be confirmed on the network.

4. Click on "Create Pool" button

When transaction is confirmed, "Create Pool" button will appear. Click on "Create Pool" button

5. Verify that your pool data is correct and proceed by clicking on "Approve Pool Token"

6. "Confirm" the transaction on your wallet and wait for it to be confirmed on the network.

7. Finally click on "Create Pool" and confirm transaction in your wallet.

8. Done! Your Pool has been deployed.

how to add BSC Network in MetaMask Wallet?

1. Click on Ethereum Network

Switch the connected blockchain by clicking on the tab saying Main Ethereum Network, we need to add the Binance Smart Chain network.

2. Scroll down until you find Custom RPC.

3. Enter in the BSC mainnet details as follows:

  • Network Name: BSC Mainnet
  • New RPC URL: https://bsc-dataseed1.binance.org/ or https://bsc-dataseed2.binance.org/
  • ChainID: 56, or 0x38 if 56 doesn’t work
  • Symbol: BNB
  • Block Explorer URL: https://bscscan.com/

4. Click Save.

You have now connected to the BSC mainnet with your Metamask Wallet.

For more information please visit: https://docs.binance.org/smart-chain/wallet/metamask.html

Guide on how to use TosDis Erc20 <> BEP20 bridge

Important Notes:

  • Wrapped DIS (BSC) contract details:
    0x57efFdE2759b68d86C544e88F7977e3314144859

    Use the above contract address to add WrappedDIS to your wallet
    Use the above contract address to find and trade WrappedDIS on Pancake swap.

  • Make sure you have correct network selected in TosDis app.

    For example:
    For ETH > BSC transit, Select ETH network in app
    For BSC > ETH transit, Select BSC network in app

  • Make sure you have some ETH and BNB in this wallet for transactions fees.

1. Open Bridge tab on Tosdis App and connect your wallet.

2. Quick tab to transit DIS token or other ERC-20 tokens.

3. Enter the amount you wish to transfer

Enter the amount you wish to transfer to BSC network and click Approve. Wait for the approve transaction to complete. (Check the transaction status from your metamask wallet).

4. Once Approved, Click transfer and approve transfer to BSC.

5. Confirm the transaction from your Wallet (prefer high gas fees).

6. Once you confirm the transaction in your wallet. Wait!! Your screen will stay like this until the transaction is complete.

7. Popup appear

Once the transaction is done, a pop up will appear and will ask you to change the blockchain network in your wallet to BSC.

8. Change your network to BSC.

9. Your transaction will appear under transact to BSC box

Your transaction will appear under transact to BSC box. "Signed" section will appear as "NO". Track your transaction from your wallet.

10. Receive Button Appear

Once 12 network confirmation are done, signed section will change to Yes and a "Receive" button under "Action" section will appear.

11. Click on Receive and claim your Wrapped DIS on BSC network.

(Preferred Gas price: 25, Gas Limit: 200000)

12. Confirm and wait for the transaction to complete. A pop up will appear once done.

13. Done!! Your DIS are now on the BSC network.
